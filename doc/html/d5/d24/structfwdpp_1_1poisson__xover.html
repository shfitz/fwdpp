Detailed Description

Simple model of crossing-over. Generates a Poisson-distributed number of recombination breakpoints with mean recrate that are uniformly-distributed between minpos and maxpos.

Examples:
diploid_fixed_sh_ind.cc, diploid_ind.cc, juvenile_migration.cc, spatialts.cc, and wfts.cc.
